Unifor 111 - Spareboard Rules (2023–2026)

Qualifications Safety Code

1.01 Qualifications for a Spareboard Operator

All Operators signing on the Spareboard List must be qualified to carry out all assignments of a Transit Operator...

Plain English: To work on the Spareboard, a driver must complete training and be able to do any operator task, even on unfamiliar routes using maps or guides.

2.01 Safety Code – Limits and Compliance

Operators must follow time limits: Max 13 driving hours, 14 on-duty hours, and 16 total hours per day, with proper off-duty breaks.

Plain English: You can't drive too long in a day. You must rest for 8 hours straight before your next shift. Violating these laws is illegal.
