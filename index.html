1.01 Qualifications for a Spareboard Operator

All Operators signing on the Spareboard List must be qualified to carry out all assignments of a Transit Operator. This means having completed all required training, including route training at any new depot.

What this means: You must be fully trained and ready to work any assignment, even at a new location. If you're trained, you can't say "I'm not familiar with this route." Maps and guides count as enough help.

2.01 Safety Code: Work Limits

Operators must comply with safety laws: max 13 hours of driving/day, 14 hours on duty, 16 hours total, with 8 hours rest. Cannot drive over 70 hours in 7 days. Reset after 36 hours off.

What this means: You can’t overwork. There are rules to protect you and others. You need breaks. Don’t take work that will push you over these limits.
